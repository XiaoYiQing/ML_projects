

The data about food in this directory are obtained from the U.S. DEPARTMENT OF AGRICULTURE website.

	fdc_id: the actual food item id.

	
	
Let's use an example for searching for nutritient fact of raw broccoli.

	1- In "food" csv file, go to line 1195, where broccoli is defined with the identification fdc_id = 321900
	
	2- In "food_nutrient" csv file, at the lines 3969 to 4075, you can find that the series of nutrient ids related to raw broccoli.
		nutrient_id
